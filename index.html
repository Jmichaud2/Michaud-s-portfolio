Hi. I'm Jean Chrislot Michaud.

I'm Jean Chrislot Michaud from Haiti, holding a Bachelor's degree in Computer Information Systems (CIS) with minors in Business Administration and Health Public Administration. In 2014, I journeyed to the US to chase my dreams.

In the US, I founded Sharing Blessings to Others, a non-profit focused on education and community empowerment. Our annual summer camp embodies our mission, offering free education, nourishing meals, computer skills, and school supplies to children and communities.

My diverse skill set, including leadership, public speaking, and hands-on engineering projects, reflects my journey. I've designed websites, managed Microsoft 365, enhanced security with Azure, and used AWS for WordPress e-commerce sites.

Eager for new challenges, I'm driven to expand my knowledge and skills. My interests span walking, reading, music, art, and movies, while helping others brings me fulfillment. I'm primed to contribute remarkably to any project or organization.
